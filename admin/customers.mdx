The customers page (`/customers`) provides a comprehensive view of your Rubik's Vintage customer base, including purchase history, contact information, and customer segmentation.

## Customer Overview

The page displays three key audience metrics:

### Total Customers (Total Clientes)
- **Icon:** Users icon
- **Shows:** Complete customer count
- **Example:** `128` customers

### New Customers This Month (Nuevos)
- **Icon:** User plus icon
- **Shows:** New customer registrations in current month
- **Example:** `+14` new this month

### VIP Customers (Clientes VIP)
- **Icon:** Star icon
- **Shows:** High-value or repeat customers
- **Example:** `08` VIP customers

## Search Customers

Use the search bar to find customers by name or email:

```text
Buscar por nombre o correo...
(Search by name or email...)
```

The search filters the customer list in real-time.

## Customer Directory Table

The main table displays customer information with the following columns:

### Customer Profile (Cliente)

**Avatar Circle:** Displays customer initials in a circular badge

**Example:** "MC" for Mariana Costa

**Customer Name:** Full name with optional VIP indicator

<Note>
VIP customers are marked with a gold star icon (⭐) next to their name.
</Note>

**Email Address:** Customer contact email displayed below name

**Example:**
```text
Mariana Costa ⭐
cliente@ejemplo.com
```

### Location (Ubicación)

Customer's city and country

**Example:** `CDMX, México`

<Note>
The location column is hidden on mobile and tablet devices to save space.
</Note>

### Order Count (Pedidos)

Number of orders placed by this customer

**Display:** Shown in a badge with border

**Example:** `03`, `05`

### Total Spent (Total Gastado)

Lifetime value - total amount spent by customer

**Format:** `$X,XXX MXN`

**Example:** `$2,500 MXN`, `$7,500 MXN`

### Profile Actions (Perfil)

Click the three-dot menu icon to access customer actions:

- View full profile
- View order history
- Send email
- Add notes
- Mark as VIP

## VIP Customer Identification

VIP customers are identified by:

1. **Gold star icon** next to their name
2. **Higher total spent** amounts
3. **Multiple orders** in their history

### VIP Benefits

While not enforced in the UI, VIP designation allows you to:

- Provide special discounts
- Offer early access to new products
- Send exclusive communications
- Prioritize their orders

<Tip>
Use the filter option to segment VIP customers for targeted marketing campaigns.
</Tip>

## Customer Segmentation

Click the **"Segmentar"** (Segment) button with filter icon to filter customers by:

- Purchase frequency
- Total spending
- Location
- VIP status
- Last order date

## Viewing Customer Details

Click the three-dot menu on any customer row to view:

### Customer Profile
- Full name and contact information
- Shipping addresses
- Account creation date
- Customer notes

### Order History
- Complete list of orders
- Order dates and amounts
- Product details
- Order status

### Customer Metrics
- Total orders placed
- Lifetime value (total spent)
- Average order value
- Last purchase date

## Customer Database

Customer information is stored in the `profiles` table in Supabase:

**Key Fields:**
- `id` - Unique customer identifier
- `first_name` - Customer first name
- `last_name` - Customer last name
- `email` - Contact email
- `created_at` - Registration date

**Related Data:**
- Orders linked via `customer_id`
- Addresses stored separately
- Purchase history calculated from orders

## Customer Communication

While email functionality is not built into the admin panel, you can:

1. Copy customer email from the table
2. Use your email client to contact customers
3. Reference order numbers from their history

<Warning>
Always respect customer privacy and follow data protection regulations (GDPR, local laws) when handling customer information.
</Warning>

## Customer Analytics

### Understanding Customer Value

**High-Value Customers:**
- Multiple orders (5+ orders)
- High total spent ($5,000+ MXN)
- Recent activity

**At-Risk Customers:**
- No recent orders (6+ months)
- Single purchase only
- Low engagement

**New Customers:**
- Registered in last 30 days
- First purchase or no purchase yet
- Opportunity for welcome campaigns

## Customer Retention

Use the customer directory to:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Top Customers">
    Sort by total spent to find your most valuable customers. Consider marking them as VIP.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor Purchase Patterns">
    Track order frequency to identify engaged customers and those who may need re-engagement.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Personalize Communication">
    Use customer history to send personalized product recommendations and exclusive offers.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Build Loyalty">
    Recognize VIP customers with special perks and early access to new vintage pieces.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The customer directory shows sample data in the UI. Real customer data will populate from your Supabase database once orders are placed.
</Note>

The admin dashboard is your command center for managing Rubik's Vintage. It provides a real-time overview of your store's performance, active orders, inventory status, and customer metrics.

## Dashboard Metrics

The dashboard displays four key performance indicators at the top:

### Total Revenue (Ingresos Totales)
Shows your total revenue with a percentage trend indicator. The green upward trend shows growth compared to the previous period.

**Example Display:** `$128,430` with `+12.5%` growth

### Active Orders (Pedidos Activos)
Displays the number of currently active orders, with a detail showing how many are pending shipment.

**Example Display:** `45 active orders` with `12 pending shipment`

### Inventory Products (Productos en Inventario)
Shows total products in stock with alerts for low-stock items that need attention.

**Example Display:** `124 products` with `8 low stock` warning

### Total Customers (Clientes Totales)
Displays your customer base size with growth trend indicator.

**Example Display:** `892 customers` with `+5.2%` growth

## Recent Orders Section

The main dashboard area features a table of recent orders with the following information:

- **Order ID:** Unique identifier (e.g., #ORD-7829)
- **Customer Name:** Full name of the customer
- **Date:** Order placement date
- **Amount:** Total order value in dollars
- **Status:** Current order state with color coding
  - **Enviado** (Shipped) - Blue badge
  - **Procesando** (Processing) - Purple badge
  - **Pendiente** (Pending) - Yellow badge

Click "Ver todos" (View all) to see complete order history.

## Inventory Status Widget

The right sidebar shows critical inventory alerts:

### Out of Stock (Sin Stock)
Number of products completely out of stock (shown in red).

### Low Stock (Stock Bajo)
Number of products with inventory below threshold (shown in yellow).

<Tip>
Click **"Gestionar Inventario"** (Manage Inventory) to quickly navigate to the inventory management page and restock items.
</Tip>

## Navigation

Use the left sidebar to access different admin sections:

- **Dashboard** - Overview (current page)
- **Inventory** - Product management
- **Orders** - Order processing
- **Customers** - Customer directory
- **Settings** - Store configuration

<Note>
The dashboard data refreshes automatically. All statistics are calculated in real-time from your store's database.
</Note>

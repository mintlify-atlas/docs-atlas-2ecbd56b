The inventory management page (`/inventory`) allows you to manage all products in your Rubik's Vintage store. You can add new pieces, edit existing products, upload images, and organize your catalog.

## Viewing Your Inventory

The inventory page displays all products in a responsive grid layout:

- **Product image** with hover effects
- **Product name** in uppercase tracking
- **Base price** in MXN (Mexican Pesos)
- **Category** name below price

### Search Functionality

Use the search bar at the top to filter products by name:

```text
Buscar pieza... (Search for a piece...)
```text

The search filters products in real-time as you type.

## Adding a New Product

Click the **"Nueva Pieza"** (New Piece) button to open the product creation form.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Product Image">
    Click the camera icon area to upload a product photograph. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF.
    
    Images are stored in Supabase Storage in the `product_image` bucket and automatically optimized.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Product Details">
    Fill in the required fields:
    
    - **Nombre** (Name): Product title (e.g., "Collar Oro Laminado")
    - **Categoría** (Category): Select from existing categories
    - **Precio Base** (Base Price): Price in MXN
    - **Descripción** (Description): Unique details about the piece
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preview Your Product">
    The right sidebar shows a live preview of how your product will appear in the store ("Vista previa del Bazar").
    
    The preview includes:
    - Product image
    - Product name
    - Formatted price
    - Shipping and authenticity icons
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish to Store">
    Click **"Publicar en la Tienda"** (Publish to Store) to add the product to your catalog.
    
    A success notification will appear when the product is saved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Product Fields Explained

### Name (Nombre)
The display name for your product. Use descriptive titles that help customers understand what they're buying.

**Examples:**
- "Collar Oro Laminado Art Deco"
- "Aretes Perla Vintage 1950s"
- "Anillo Plata con Circonias"

### Category (Categoría)
Products must be assigned to a category. Categories are pre-defined in your database and help organize your catalog.

Common categories:
- Collares (Necklaces)
- Aretes (Earrings)
- Anillos (Rings)
- Pulseras (Bracelets)

### Base Price (Precio Base)
Price in Mexican Pesos (MXN). Enter numeric value only (e.g., `1250.00`).

<Note>
The price is displayed with MXN currency formatting throughout the store. Shipping costs are added separately during checkout.
</Note>

### Description (Descripción)
Optional field for unique details about the piece:
- Era or time period
- Materials and craftsmanship
- Condition notes
- Historical significance

## Editing Existing Products

Hover over any product card in the grid to reveal action buttons:

### Edit Button (Pencil Icon)
Click the pencil icon to open the edit modal with pre-filled product information.

The edit modal allows you to:
- Replace the product image
- Update name, category, and price
- Modify description
- Save changes with **"Guardar Cambios"** (Save Changes)

### Delete Button (Trash Icon)
Click the trash icon to remove a product from your catalog.

<Warning>
Deletion requires confirmation and is permanent. The product will be removed from your database.
</Warning>

## Image Management

### Uploading Images

1. Click the upload area (camera icon)
2. Select an image file from your computer
3. Preview appears immediately
4. Image is uploaded to Supabase Storage on form submission

### Image Requirements

- **Aspect ratio:** 3:4 recommended for product cards
- **Format:** JPEG, PNG, or GIF
- **Size:** Images are stored with random filenames to prevent conflicts

### Changing Images

In the edit modal, click the existing image to upload a new one. The old image remains in storage but the product will display the new URL.

## Product Categories

Categories are managed in the `categories` database table and displayed in dropdown menus.

### Viewing by Category

Each product card shows its category name below the price in small, uppercase text.

<Tip>
Contact your database administrator to add or modify product categories. Categories cannot be managed from the admin UI.
</Tip>

## Inventory Statistics

The page header shows:

```text
X piezas en colección
(X pieces in collection)
```text

This count updates automatically as you add or remove products.

## Success Notifications

After adding or editing a product, a success notification appears at the top of the page confirming the action was completed.

## Low Stock Alerts

While not visible on the inventory page, low stock warnings appear on the dashboard. The inventory page focuses on product content management rather than stock levels.

<Note>
All product data is stored in the `products` table in your Supabase database. Images are stored separately in the `product_image` storage bucket.
</Note>
